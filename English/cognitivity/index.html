Save phone's memory, use our brain memory

As science and technology continues fastforward, we tend to build a habit of using devices to record things that we used to just use our brain. I strongly support we adapt ourselves to technologies at the same time I also love to train my brain to do the necessary chores.

The idea comes from my personal experience of learning English as a Chinese native. The best way to learn CHINESE is to memorize the charactors and the best way is VISUALIZATION. To learn ENGLISH, on the other hand, I discovered that LOGIC is the most foundamental whereas memory only places a less significant impact.

So, when it comes to small things like chores, I personally try to memorize numbers (eg. phone numbers), alphabets (eg. English words), shapes (eg. routes)

Aging replaces memory, what should we keep?

"It is real that my memory function is declining, believe me," are you one thinking like this? If yes, I am sorry that I would have to disagree even if you are a scientist.

This is what I have noticed so far based on my personal experience: compared with when I was a minor whose role was mainly full-time student and somebody's kid, as an adult, I have more tasks to process simutaneously, consequently, my memory has been divided to store MORE TYPES of fragments but the total amount of memory in fact probably became larger.

Our brain is our calculator

When in the supermarket or online department store, we would always have to calculate the discount and the total.

Very often, online platform would already provide us with their technology to let us know how far we are to get to the next level of grand discount. It is our instinct to simply add one more item into the shopping cart and let the computer do the work for us. When we do it, we lose a chance of training our cognitivity.

So next time, use the time that you use to click between buttons to calculate first by yourself then to confirm your calculation with the computer later. Who knows what we might get?

Be cheap, check super tickers

Grocery store frequently have to clear out products that are about to expire. When in cases like this, the wholesaler would put super sticker on top of the product. Sometimes, some products always come with superstickers.

We can visit more grocery supermarkets, remember price differences of different stores. Calculate the differences, check each time, how big the discount actually is.

Learn new languge, get the tongue rolling

I noticed that as I age, the chance of my tongue and mouth to move as swiftly and accurately is getting higher.

I can not pronounce words accurately as I used to. I cann't speak as sound and clear as I used to when I was a kid.

By far, I have not concluded the cause and a solution yet, but I feel that when I learn a new language, my memory and instinct of speaking clearly and acurately is stronger.

Shake shake not sit sit

I am not sure how is replacing sitting, laying, driving, with walking, running, or biing will help with my cognitivity.

These activities just feel way too good. So I intend to believe it is helpful in slowing down the speed of me-towards-dumbness.

The first 30 minutes of exercise is always so challenging on the brain. Once we hit that 30 minutes, the whole world seems must brighter and happier.
